About RPS

RESEARCH PUBLISHING (S) PTE. LTD. (Singapore)

Research Publishing Services (RPS) is a publishing company headquartered in Singapore with facilities in Singapore and Chennai. RPS offers state-of-the-art technology-driven products and services to conference organizers, societies, and university presses across the globe. At RPS, we strive to deliver products and services that suit each customer's unique needs by blending creativity and originality with business efficiency.

Fired by a passion for business excellence and driven by sound human values, RPS continues to grow based on its capabilities. We at RPS deliver on commitments, a practice that has transformed itself into a hallmark of RPS's business procedures and reason for its success story. We nurture new technological strengths, while enlarging the domain expertise substantially. Our expert workflows based on global best practices translate into a significant reduction in turnaround times.

RPS is strongly driven by values such as Quality, Commitment, Discipline, Learning, Integrity, Respect, Teamwork, Customer-orientation and Trust. Research Publishing Services was founded by publishing experts, who have worked in international publishing, production, and marketing for over a decade.
